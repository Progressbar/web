hackerspace progressbar.sk.

We are first physical place in Bratislava for meeting hackers,
digital artists, geeks, hardware wizards
and likeminded people.

We are located in the center of Bratislava
at Cukrová 14, near the main bus station Mlynské Nivy.

We are regularly held interesting lectures,
workshops and meetings of various communities.

Most of events organized at the hackerspace
are open for public and for free.
You are welcome visit to us!
